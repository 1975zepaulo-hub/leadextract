FAQ

How is this different from the old 24-hour delivery?

You now extract directly. Type your keyword, click Extract, and results appear on your screen within minutes — no waiting for email delivery.

What is the no-website filter?

It filters results to only show businesses that have no website listed on Google Maps. Perfect for web designers pitching brand-new sites. You reach them by phone, WhatsApp or text.

What countries can I search?

50+ countries — Nigeria, Ghana, USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, Germany, and more. Just type the location in your search.

Can I run multiple searches?

Yes. Your monthly limit is the total number of leads extracted, not the number of searches. Run as many searches as you want until you hit your plan limit.

Do I get emails in the results?

Yes on Starter, Growth, and Agency plans. Free plan includes all fields except email extraction.
